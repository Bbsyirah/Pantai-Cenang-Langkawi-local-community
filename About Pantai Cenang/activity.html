ACTIVITIES

PANTAI CENANG

On Pantai Cenang, there are various operators who provide chances for water sports. Jet ski trips, water ski adventures, kayaking, speedboat thrill rides, and banana boating are among the available activities. However, a majority of them lack proper licenses, use substandard material, and disregard even the most fundamental safety precautions.

Jet Ski

Jetski new 2 hours tour will give you the perfect adrenaline and fun jetski experience exploring some surrounding islands of Langkawi. Now only RM499, If sharing for 2 pax, average per pax RM249.50

Kayaking

How greater time to approach or end your day than with the adrenaline of kayaking. Run through the Langkawi Sea as fast as you can. Alternately, relax a while and paddle your own around to get a closer look at nature. Whatever happens, it's a fantastic time!

Helicopter Tour

Take your adventure to the skies in this helicopter joyride taster! See Cenang beach with a bird’s eye view in an experience like no other!

Para Sailing

Will be lifted into the air and pulled to the boat. The para sail features all necessary safety equipment, including belts that match your size and life jackets, and can accommodate up to three passengers. A bird's eye view of the region will be yours as you float in the air around 500 feet over the ocean.

Banana Boat

With the price RM15/person and they will bring you until "middle of sea" and make you fall from the boat. The maximum is 7 people/boat.
