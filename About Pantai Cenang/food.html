FOODS

When visiting Pantai Cenang, it is a must to find a good restaurants that serves good foods. So, here is the top budget, mid-range and upscale restaurants or cafe that can be found in Pantai Cenang, Langkawi.




Category: Budget, Cuisine: Mexican

With options for air-conditioned indoor or outdoor sitting, El Toro Mexican Bar & Grill is situated in the Cenang Mall. While spending your evening in your preferred vacation spot, our cocktail bar transports you to a tropical paradise. The El Toro bar is the ideal location for entertainment and leisure, whether you want to drink an ice-cold beer with friends while lounging in the shade and taking in the warm tropical breeze or a mocktail with your significant other. Wide range of drinks and mocktails with a Latin American vibe are available from our international bartending team.


ADDRESS:

CENANG MALL, FF4, LOT 2605, JALAN PANTAI CENANG, PANTAI CENANG, 07000 LANGKAWI, KEDAH




Category: Budget, Cuisine: Indian & Malay

Tomato Nasi Kandar is situated across from the new Cenang Mall and close to the AB Motel. Due to the wonderful food and affordable prices, it is a very popular restaurant in Pantai Cenang with both visitors and residents.


ADDRESS:

JALAN PANTAI CENANG, PANTAI CENANG, 07000 LANGKAWI, KEDAH.




Category: Budget, Cuisine: Seafood BBQ

A front-side open-air dining area is available at Difiqa. Both candle-lit tables and individual seating areas have shades. In the evening, the location appears fairly good. Customers can choose their seafood from the frozen glass tank near the entrance by weight, and it will be grilled. Additionally, they provide grilled chicken and other regional cuisine. This roadside eatery on Jalan Pantai Cenang is reasonably priced and is situated on the side of the road. They serves dinner only.


ADDRESS:

2, JALAN PANTAI CENANG, KAMPUNG LUBOK BUAYA, 07000 LANGKAWI, KEDAH




Category: Mid-Range, Cuisine: Thai

This wonderful Thai restaurant has an entrance from the road and is situated directly on the Cenang beach length. There are dining options with fantastic views of the beach and water both inside and outside. The Thai cuisine offers a wide variety of options for fish, poultry, prawns, lobsters, crab, squid, and other seafood. Dinner and lunch are served.


ADDRESS:

MAIN STREET, PANTAI CENANG, 07000 LANGKAWI, KEDAH




Category: Upscale, Cuisine: Malay & Continental

This restaurant is well-known for offering a breathtaking view of the setting sun because it is situated directly on the rocky cliff that divides Pantai Cenang and Pantai Tengah beaches. It is divided into three parts: a restaurant, a cocktail lounge with a bar in the middle, and a garden area with sitting by the lake. The water is clearly visible from each of the three sections. The continental and tapas meals, as well as the seafood preparations in the traditional Malay style, are of the highest calibre.


ADDRESS:

LOT 63 & 40, CENANG, MUKIM KEDAWANG, LEBUH PANTAI, 0700 PULAU LANGKAWI, KEDAH
