SHOPPING


Cenang Mall

The Cenang Mall shopping complex, which opened on December 23, 2012, in Pantai Cenang, Langkawi, is the Ultimate Shopping Experience. Since then, it has grown to be Langkawi's most prominent and modern retail mall in the laid-back leisure atmosphere of Langkawi thanks to the mall's vivid and modern conceptual design, which guaranteed a pleasant and leisurely lifestyle by the beach.


This two storey large shopping complex took up a portion of the 11,310 square meter has come up right on the main street (Jalan Pantai Cenang) having provision for 42 shops, several restaurants, elevators & escalators as well as amenities like a prayer room, money changer, and restrooms with access for those with disabilities.The tenants of Cenang Mall include predominately well-known international businesses including Starbucks, 7-Eleven, Guardians, KFC, McDonald's Kiosk, Maybank, etc.


This landmark delivers the most dynamic lifestyle experience ever and is especially created to allow customers to shop with convenience and comfort in a fully air-conditioned atmosphere. It is a landmark for Langkawians in general and Pantai Cenang locals in particular.
